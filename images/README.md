# Images Folder

## Required Images:

1. **profile.jpg** (300x300px recommended)
   - Your professional headshot
   - Square format works best
   - High quality, well-lit photo

2. **project1.jpg** (16:9 aspect ratio recommended)
   - Screenshot of your E-Commerce Platform
   - 800x450px or similar resolution

3. **project2.jpg** (16:9 aspect ratio recommended)
   - Screenshot of your Task Management App
   - 800x450px or similar resolution

4. **project3.jpg** (16:9 aspect ratio recommended)
   - Screenshot of your Weather Dashboard
   - 800x450px or similar resolution

## Image Guidelines:
- Use JPG or PNG format
- Keep file sizes under 1MB for fast loading
- Use descriptive, professional images
- Ensure images are your own work or properly licensed
